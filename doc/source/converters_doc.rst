***********************
Legacy ESC/P2 Converter
***********************

External programs like ESC converters are written in C or C++.
This is the choice of the programmers depending on their preferences or by desire to
hide their work and make their project not modifiable.

These programs **do not meet our expected quality standards and are gradually
being replaced** by our own implementations, which are more compliant with
historical standards and deliver better performance.
